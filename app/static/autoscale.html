Autoscaling Under Load

Fire concurrent queries and watch latency improve as Lakebase scales compute dynamically

How it works

Configure the autoscaling range (1-8 CU), then fire waves of concurrent queries — heavy (4-table JOIN + aggregation), medium (2-table JOIN), and light (single-table GROUP BY).

Under burst load, Lakebase automatically scales compute up. Watch per-query latency decrease in real-time as more CUs come online.
